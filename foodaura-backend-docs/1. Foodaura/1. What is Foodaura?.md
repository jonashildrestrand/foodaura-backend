# What is Foodaura?

Foodaura is a food companion for households who want to eat with purpose. It combines meal planning, recipe discovery, and smart shopping — all tailored to who you are, what you're trying to achieve, and who you're cooking for.

Most people know they want to eat better, but figuring out *what* to eat, *how much*, and *which foods* actually move the needle toward their goals is overwhelming. That complexity multiplies in a household where everyone has different needs — a partner trying to build muscle, a child who's a picky eater, or a flatmate on a different schedule. Foodaura handles this by building meal plans around each person's nutritional profile, then scaling recipes intelligently — for the whole household when eating together, or for individuals on the days they're on their own. And for those who want full control, plans can be built manually too.

The result is a weekly routine that goes from goals to grocery list: discover recipes that fit your household, plan your week around how you actually live, and shop with a single consolidated list — without spending hours cross-referencing nutrition or cooking separate meals for everyone.

## What Foodaura isn't

**Not a calorie tracker.** Foodaura is about building sustainable eating habits, not obsessing over numbers. Nutrition is a guide, not a scoreboard.

**Not a meal kit service.** Foodaura doesn't sell or deliver food. It helps you plan and shop smarter with what's already available to you.

**Not a diet app.** Foodaura doesn't prescribe restrictive programs or fad diets. It works with your goals — whatever they are — and adapts to you.
